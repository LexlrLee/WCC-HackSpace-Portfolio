Zhengyang Li

With the advent of digital technology and the virtual age, one inevitably enters the age of simulation. Classes, values and even ideologies are swallowed up by the black hole of mass media such as advertising, news and entertainment, and reality is absent in simulation.

In this context I have always explored the symbiotic relationship between technology and people. Based on the research of mysticism, I have created works on meditation, surrealism, cross-species perception and many other genres. In my previous work, I used to use computer vision and multiple programming languages to create new media artworks in order to enhance the perception of the viewer.
